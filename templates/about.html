About Us

Our Mission

AviationHub is dedicated to empowering aspiring pilots by providing free, accessible resources to help them achieve their aviation goals. Whether you're a high school student dreaming of flight or well into your pilot training, we're here to support you.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a passionate aviation enthusiast, AviationHub was born out of a desire to create a comprehensive online platform for aspiring pilots. As a computer science student with a deep love for flying, I recognized the need for a centralized hub of information and support for those sharing my passion.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to resource for aspiring pilots worldwide. We strive to create a supportive community where pilots can connect, share experiences, and learn from each other.

AviationHub is more than just a website; it's a starting point for your aviation journey.
